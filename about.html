Shaping a Sustainable Future Through Innovation

For over three decades, Rosehill has led the way in creating innovative infrastructure products that make our world safer, more sustainable, and more efficient.

Engineering Excellence Since 1988

The infrastructure challenges of the modern world require solutions that deliver both performance and environmental responsibility. Operating from our UK headquarters in Sowerby Bridge, we've established ourselves as a global leader in engineered infrastructure solutions.

From road safety systems and railway products to modular drainage and recreational surfaces, our products are trusted worldwide. By manufacturing our own polyurethane binders and controlling the entire production process, we ensure exceptional quality across our entire product range.

Committed to the Circular Economy

Our commitment to the circular economy drives everything we do. Through innovative manufacturing processes, we transform recycled materials into high-performance infrastructure products that meet rigorous industry standards.
