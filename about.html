Advanced Materials & Infrastructure Solutions

For over three decades, Rosehill has led the way in engineering advanced materials that protect, connect and perform in the world's toughest environments.

Engineering Excellence Since 1988

The modern world's infrastructure and materials challenges require solutions that deliver both performance and environmental responsibility. Operating from our UK headquarters in Sowerby Bridge, we've established ourselves as a global leader in advanced materials and engineered infrastructure solutions.

From specialty chemicals and polymer systems to road safety and recreational surfaces, our products are trusted worldwide. By manufacturing our own polyurethane binders and controlling the entire production process, we ensure exceptional quality across our materials and infrastructure product range.

Our Specialist Divisions

Our infrastructure and materials divisions deliver tailored solutions that meet the unique demands of each market, exemplifying British engineering excellence.

Infrastructure Solutions

Road Safety Solutions

Surface-mounted systems that enhance road safety for all users. Designed for quick installation and long-term performance, they support safer, more efficient transport networks.

Rail Infrastructure

Robust, low-maintenance components that improve safety across the rail network, including level crossings and anti-trespass panels engineered for long service life.

Defence & Security

Modular ballistic protection and hostile vehicle mitigation systems. Developed for high-risk environments where speed, flexibility, and reliability are essential.

Drainage Solutions

Sustainable water management systems that combine effective drainage performance with circular economy principles for modern infrastructure projects.
